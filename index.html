Maori protests throughout NZ

Maori protesters have been rummaging through the streets of New Zealand on a shocking day, demanding that their language be restored.As the police continue to try to deal with them, they are holding signs that read, "It's a right we don't have to beg for."

The government has decided to debate the current situation on behalf of the Maori people.According to Smith Jackson, "They are essentially just disrupting."Tohu Nikau, on the other hand, has a completely different point of view."This is our language you have taken away from us, and we shall never let go of," he declares.
