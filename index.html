Maori protests throughout NZ

It has been a shocking day at New Zealand as Maori protestants have been rummaging the streets, asking for their language back. They hold signs saying "It's a right we don't have to beg" as police are still trying to handle them

On the Maoris behalf, the government has decided to debate on the current status. As Smith Jackson says "They are all but just disrupting". At the same time Tohu Nikau has a whole different perspective. He says that "This is our language that you have taken away from us, and we shall never let go of."
